The Designer

My Story, My Passion

From a young age, I saw fashion as more than just clothing; it's a language, a form of art, a silent expression of confidence. 'Hands of Fia' was born from a desire to blend timeless elegance with modern audacity.

My design philosophy is simple: create pieces that empower. Each collection is inspired by the stories of strong individuals, by nature's geometry, and by the vibrant pulse of the city.

The Design Process

From Concept to Creation

1. Consultation

It begins with your story. We meet to discuss your vision, inspiration, and desires in a personal consultation.

2. Sketch & Fabric

Ideas take shape. We present initial sketches and source the perfect, luxurious materials for your approval.

3. Creation & Fitting

Our atelier brings the design to life. We hold meticulous fittings to ensure a flawless and comfortable fit.

4. The Reveal

Your one-of-a-kind piece is complete, ready to be worn and to empower your unique story.
